KYC and Identity Verification

Identity verification is required for financial transactions and account security.

Accepted Documents

  • National ID card
  • Passport
  • Driver’s license

Document Requirements

  • Clear full name
  • Address
  • ID/document number
  • Clear frontal photo

Common Reasons Verification Fails

  • Name does not match the application
  • Name is entered in the wrong format
  • Incorrect or incomplete information
  • Image is blurred or unclear
  • Document is expired or expires too soon
  • Applicant does not meet company conditions

Age Requirement

Users must be at least 18 years old and not more than 70 years old, subject to company review and discretion.

Re-application

If a user has already registered and completed identity verification, they should log in with the same email and should not apply again with a new submission.
