Bank Account Verification

The bank account used must match the name used for identity verification.

Common Reasons Bank Verification Fails

  • Name on the bank account does not match the verified identity
  • Bank account number is incomplete or incorrect
  • Identity verification has not been approved yet
  • Incorrect supporting document attached
  • Image is unclear or incomplete
  • The bank account is not owned by the customer

Important Rule

Only bank accounts that belong to the customer and match the verified identity can be approved.
