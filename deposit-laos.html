Laos Deposit Process

1. Click “Deposit”.

2. Select the account you wish to deposit into.

3. Enter the deposit amount.

Note: The minimum deposit is $50. The system will automatically calculate and display the equivalent amount after entering the value in Lao Kip.

4. Scan the QR code or transfer via bank account. Once completed, please provide the following information in the system:

  • 4.1 Enter the sender’s bank account number (the account used for the transfer).
  • 4.2 Select the transfer date according to the payment slip.
  • 4.3 Upload the transfer slip to the system.

Important: The bank account number and transfer date provided must match the details shown on the uploaded payment slip.
