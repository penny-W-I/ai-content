IUX Account Types

Standard Account

  • Low spread account
  • Leverage up to 1:3000
  • Free swap
  • Minimum deposit: $50

Raw Account

  • Spread starts from 0.0 pips
  • Leverage up to 1:3000
  • Commission: $7 per lot
  • Free swap
  • Minimum deposit: $200

Pro Account

  • Spread starts from 0.1 pips
  • Leverage up to 1:3000
  • Commission: 0
  • Free swap
  • Minimum deposit: $200

Cent Account

For Cent accounts, 100 usc = 1 USD.

The maximum withdrawal amount is 10,000 usc per transaction. If the limit is exceeded, withdrawals can be made in increments of 10,000 usc.
