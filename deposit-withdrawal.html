Deposit and Withdrawal Information

General Deposit Information

IUX provides multiple deposit methods, including electronic payments and other supported payment channels available in the deposit menu.

Deposit Conditions

  • Deposits must be made in the name of the account holder only.
  • Any bank account can be used for deposit, but the name must match the verified identity.
  • If the deposit is made by someone else, profits may be void and only the original deposited amount may be returned.

Deposit and Withdrawal Fees

IUX does not charge additional deposit or withdrawal fees. Any fee charged depends on the payment intermediary or bank.

Deposit and Withdrawal Limits

  • Standard account minimum deposit: $50
  • Raw account minimum deposit: $200
  • Pro account minimum deposit: $200
  • Minimum withdrawal: $10
  • Maximum deposit per transaction: $20,000
  • Maximum withdrawal per transaction: $20,000

Processing Time

  • QR code deposits are credited immediately after successful payment.
  • Withdrawals normally take 5–15 minutes and no more than 24 hours.

If Deposit Is Missing

Please provide your email, MT5 account number, and deposit slip for investigation.

If Withdrawal Exceeds 24 Hours

Please provide your email, MT5 account number, and withdrawal amount for further checking.
