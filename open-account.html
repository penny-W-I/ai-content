How to Open an IUX Account

To open an MT5 trading account, customers must first register on the website and complete identity verification.

  1. Register for membership on the IUX website.
  2. Complete identity verification.
  3. Wait for approval confirmation by email.
  4. Click the “Add MT5 Account” button.
  5. Select the account type you want to open.

Trading Account Limit

Customers can open up to 6 real trading accounts in total across Standard, Raw, and Pro accounts. Demo accounts can also be opened up to 6 accounts.

Account Closure

A real trading account can only be closed when the balance is 0. If there is a remaining balance, it must first be internally transferred or withdrawn.
